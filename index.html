About SRKR Coding Club

We are a community of passionate coders dedicated to fostering technical excellence and innovation at SRKR Engineering College.

Skill Development

We provide workshops, training sessions, and resources to help students enhance their coding skills and stay updated with the latest technologies.

Competitions

We organize coding competitions like IconCoderz and participate in inter-college events to showcase talent and foster healthy competition.

Community

We build a supportive community where students can collaborate, share knowledge, and grow together as developers.
